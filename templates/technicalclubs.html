Clubs are an integral part of VIT Pune. There are various clubs, working for a wide range of goals. From technical to cultural, VIT’s Clubs Culture has no boundaries. This page will help you understand what the Technical Clubs at VIT exactly do and help you find the club of your interest.
TECHNICAL CLUBS

The Robotics Forum

The Robotics Forum is a group of highly dedicated individuals, right from First Year to Final Year Undergraduates, to Professors and HODs, working passionately in the field of robotics to take the college to new heights in this field. The forum focuses on promoting interdependence between various domains.  It also focuses on the principle of 'development through knowledge transfer', within the club as well as the college.

Computer Society of India, Student Chapter

It's a community of IT geeks with the vision to share ideas on technology and advancement of theory to inspire and integrate students into the IT community, aiming at career enhancement and knowledge sharing among all categories of IT professionals.

Developer Student Clubs

It is a University-based community group for students interested in Google developer technologies. UG students or Graduates with an interest in growing as a developer are welcomed. By joining a DSC, students gain knowledge in a peer-to-peer learning environment and build solutions for local businesses and their communities. 

GedIT

GedIT is a club organized by the IT & MCA students that mainly focuses on training and developing the coding skills of fellow members who have joined the club for learning various upcoming technologies in the realm of Coding and Design.

Griffin

Griffin is an association of aviation enthusiasts, aspiring pilots, and RC modelers. The club designs manufacture and tests 'fixed‑wing' Unmanned Aerial Vehicles (UAVs). They also participate in national and inter‑college aeromodelling competitions and workshops, primarily focusing on SAE INDIA Southern section event along with the Boeing aeromodelling competition held across the country. 

IEEE Student Branch, VIT Pune

IEEE is not just a technical society, it is an experience! Do you have a curious mind? Of course, we know where to place one! Being a stage for lifelong experiments of all those keen ideas of our very own tech enthusiasts, IEEE Student Branch VIT Pune has grown into a huge community of extremely talented and like‑minded individuals.

International Society of Automation

ISA is a non‑profit professional association founded in 1945 to create a better world through automation. The organization develops widely‑used global standards; provides education and training; publishes books and technical articles; hosts conferences and exhibits.

Microsoft Learn Student Club

MLSC is a technical club, under the wing of Microsoft Learn Student Ambassadors, showcasing and nurturing talents under the organization's well-known and esteemed name to carry forth and express its legacy.  The club conduct events, workshops, hackathons along with some social initiatives in technology, inspiring students to pursue knowledge, in their unique ways.

The Chemical Engineering Students Association

The Chemical Engineering Students Association (ChESA) was formed in the year 2007 with a view to bridging the gap between the classroom theoretical academics and Industrial expectations from the students by means of a variety of Academic, Social and Curricular Activities. The Clubs aims at the overall development of the students.

Team Endurance Racing

Team Endurance Racing is a collegiate technical club and official BAJA team of VIT Pune. It participates in the annually held BAJA SAE India intending to design, manufacture and race a single‑seater All‑Terrain Vehicle that can withstand the harshest elements of rough terrain.  This vehicle is tested for its acceleration, braking performance, maneuverability, and suspension strength along with Design, Cost, and Sales Evaluation.

Team Taurus

Team Taurus is a group of engineers who are zealous about Go‑karting. The team intends to present a competent kart at national-level events every year. It considers vehicle dynamics diligently to design every part involved. After analysis and optimization, actual manufacturing is done.  The team tests its kart to its full limits to ensure top performance and mends the flaws if any. 

Technocrats Club

This Club focuses on learning through project in the field such as Android Development, Web Development, Game Design as well as Graphic Designing. Technocrats is the only Game Development Club in VIT. They also take workshop on this project to teach the interested students.

Trident Labs

Trident Labs is a student‑run club, where the student community comes together and works in the research and development of autonomous technologies. This team provides a platform to explore new avenues by conducting various events and workshops. These workshops witness dignitaries and industry professionals who provide in‑depth knowledge and industrial application of current trenchant technologies. 

Veloce Racing

Veloce Racing is the official Formula Student Team of VIT, Pune. Formula Student is the world's largest engineering design competition for students, aimed at building a Formula-style racecar with rules provided by the organizing committee, ensuring safe on‑site operation of the car.  Students go from conceiving the ideas they want to implement in the car, to designing, manufacturing, and testing the car in less than a year.
