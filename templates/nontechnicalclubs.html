Clubs are the integral part of VIT Pune. There are various clubs, working towards a wide range of goals. From technical to cultural, VIT’s Clubs Culture had no boundaries. This page will help you understand what the Non‑Technical Clubs and the Event Bodies at VIT exactly do and help you find the club of your interest..
NON-TECHNICAL CLUBS

Abhivriddhi ‑
Student Training and
Development Cell

Abhivriddhi ‑ Student Training and Development Cell is a student‑led cell, run by the students and for the students. It aims to promote and support student development by providing high‑quality educational training programs.
.

Entrepreneurship Development Cell VIT Pune

Entrepreneurship Development Cell VIT Pune constantly endeavours to provide a supportive and nurturing environment to campus startups in terms of helping in the development of the business plan, guidance in getting finance and networking opportunities with successful entrepreneurs and investors.

Zephyr

Zephyr is the official dance club of VIT Pune. It is a club of dedicated dancers who not only strive to get better at the art but also encourage creativity, consistency and teamwork. The Club believes in "wishing, working and winning! "

Yuj

Yuj is the official Yoga club of VIT Pune. 
We initiate to unite people and help them unite their body & mind. The word 'Yoga' is derived from the Sanskrit root 'Yuj', meaning 'to unite'. Yuj initiates to unite people and to help them unite with their bodies and mind.

The Investment Forum

The primary vision of The Investment Forum is to make the world of finance and investment‑related knowledge accessible to the students. The Investment Forum aims at training the students to take their financial decisions. The clubs stand to create awareness about the national and international market and make the students aware of the different investment options available and how to avail them efficiently.

Antariksh ‑ The Astronomy Club

The club welcomes all those who are interested in astronomy and fields related to it. The club appreciates your passion and feeds your curiosity through various activities throughout the year such as workshops, lecture series, talks and interactive sessions with experts and engineers from prestigious institutes related to Astronomy.

Diva Club

"She had a flair for doing things in a very elegant way" The ‘Diva Club’, VIT Pune believes that a woman is a full circle, within her is the power to create, nurture and transform. This is a club who stands for women empowerment and is ready to break all the stereotypes!

Ekasutram ‑ The Mathematics Club

Ekasutram is the only mathematics club of VIT Pune. It deals with the application of mathematics in daily life as well as in complex situations. The club focuses on inculcating a mathematical approach to face problems efficiently, leading to more accurate results.

The Economic Society

Economic society strives to extend economic concepts beyond the realms of the theory by blending it with technology using data analysis and its applications. We implement real‑life business‑centric models with the help of upcoming technologies through a series of multidisciplinary events that would help students enhance their job prospects.

Mirage Campus Film Society

The Club is envisioned to form a community of Film Enthusiasts within the college where students can discuss and share their opinions freely and bond with other people on their shared love of cinema. It tries to achieve this by conducting regular screenings of good movies and conducting an open forum after the screening where students can express their opinions about the movie.

Personality Development Club

Personality Development Club visions to develop Character, Competence and Culture in the lives of students and help them live a meaningful life. The club helps students to deal sensitivity and self‑awareness in thought, words and action and save themselves from self‑destructing habits and addiction that harm body and mind.

Pi Editorial Board

The Pi Editorial Board is a culmination of writers, artists and poets from across the college. Founded in 1994, it is perhaps the oldest club in college. They're the people behind the annual Crescendo magazine. The Pi Editorial board works in three vernacular sections, English/Technical, Hindi and Marathi, as well as a journalistic initiative, Unplugged.

Speakers Club

Speakers Club endeavors to make students excel in oratory skills while boosting their confidence and giving them an edge over others. This club ensures a place to cultivate ideas and express them and provides everyone with a platform to experience concepts that would otherwise be alien to them.

TEDxVITPune

TEDxVITPune is an independent TED event organized in the college. Starting in 2015, TEDxVITPune was among the first TEDx events in Pune and has grown to be a premier platform for furthering TED’s mission of ‘Ideas Worth Spreading’, within its local community. Over the past five events, TEDxVITPune has brought together visionaries, thinkers, and doers of different disciplines from around the country.

VClick

Vclick club is responsible for all the moments that are being captured in each event and fest conducted in the college. The club tries to click the best photographs of every event and further sends those pictures to the respective event heads so that they can upload them on the website and social media platforms.

Vishwakarma Expression Hub

Vishwakarma Expression Hub is a club that brings together the sought-after domains of poetry, prose, writings, narratives, photography, videography, graphics designing, and aesthetics. The only club in VIT to cater to so many domains together. It is dedicated to liberating the photographer, videographer, writer, poet, painter, and illustrator in oneself. 

VIT Pune MUN Society

VIT Pune MUN Society provides a platform for the exchange of ideas, debates and discussions which help in creating global citizenship and organizational skill development with ample analytical understanding and oratory prowess. It hosts various activities as well as training workshops to help inculcate these versatilities and to increase general awareness among the youth.

VIT Pune Model United Nations

VIT Pune Model United Nations is one of the premier MUN conferences which aims to bring about change through active dialogue among youth on various local and global issues. For the past four years, VPM has consistently delivered great debates and experiences to truly revolutionize the youth’s outlook towards the global political as well as social scenarios. 

The Quiz Society

The Quiz Society was founded in 2018. The club intends to inculcate a passion for quizzing, gaining knowledge and sharing it among a broad spectrum of people ranging from college students to corporates spread across the nation.The club hosts various quizzes in any given academic year and also runs periodic quizzes on various social media platforms.

VICULP ‑ VIT Cultural Production

Theatre is VICULP's expertise!
The club brings to you, Art, Craft and Acting endeavours together and gives your talents a chance to tell one really well written story and create a beautiful production of it. A huge proportion of this goes into brainstorming new and audience‑engaging events to narrate the story. Collectively all the club members strive to tell the most genuine versions of their stories!

VishwaConclave

VishwaConclave is amongst India’s first truly multi‑disciplinary student‑centric conferences organized by the students of the college. It is an initiative launched in 2019 and it emphasizes curating an inspirational dialogue from exemplary orators who have excelled in domains centered on nation‑building along with facilitating their interaction with the student community.
